About Me

My background, skills and hobbies...

Hi There! I am a UX/UI designer living in DC. My goal is to make things simple, beautiful and easy to use. I'm an energetic person passionate about more than just digital world. I love interior design, cooking and snowboarding. For me, life is always about learning, exploring, and experiencing different cultures around the world.

UI/UX Design

Create slick workflow and stuning UI for both web & mobile apps. Always pursue the best user experience with excellent business balance.

Usability Testing and Analysis

HCI background. Experienced in planning and conducting usability test. Familiar with various prototyping and analysis tools, such as Omnigraffle, Framer, Axure, Optimal Workshop etc.

Visual Design

Passionate about iconography, typography, color, space and texture. Familiar with design principles. Expert-level of Adobe Creative Suite.

Front end & Theme
